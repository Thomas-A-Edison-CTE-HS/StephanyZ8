Almond Croissants

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • 1/2 cup of almond paste
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F
  2. Roll out the puff pastry
  3. Cut it into triangles
  4. Spread a thin layer of almond paste on each triangle
  5. Roll each triangle from the base to the tip and curve into a crescent shape
  6. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with powdered sugar
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes until it’s golden brown
  8. Cool and Enjoy!
