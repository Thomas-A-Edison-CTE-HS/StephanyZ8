Almond Croissants

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • 1/2 cup of almond paste
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
    •

    Instructions

    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F
    2. Roll out the puff pastry
    3. Cut it into triangles
    4. Spread a thin layer of almond paste on each triangle
    5. Roll each triangle from the base to the tip and curve into a crescent shape
    6. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with powdered sugar
    7. Bake for 15-20 minutes until it’s golden brown
    8. Cool and Enjoy!

    Sourdough Bread

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup sourdough starter
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
    •

    Instructions

    1. Mix the starter, water, and 3 cups of flour in a large bowl
    2. Add salt, and remaining flour 1 tablespoon at a time until they are fully incorporated
    3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 15 minutes
    4. Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl
    5. Cover it and let it rise for about 5 hours
    6. Shape the dough into a loaf
    7. Leave to rest for 30 minutes
    8. preheat your oven to 450°F
    9. bake for about 30 minutes until it's golden brown
    10. Enjoy!

    Classic Carrot Cake

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
    •

    Instructions

    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F
    2. Mix together the flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl
    3. Stir in the oil and eggs until smooth
    4. Fold in the carrots, pineapple, and walnuts
    5. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan
    6. Bake for 30-35 minutes
    7. Allow the cake to cool before frosting with cream cheese frosting
    8. Enjoy!
